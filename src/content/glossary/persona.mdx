---
title: persona
definition: An archetype that summarizes a group of people that is interesting to the project
---

Personas are a powerful tool for building products.

They are fictional characters, created based on research or assumptions, with the goal of creating a set of people that the project caters to.

These characters serve as archetypes that can be used when ideating about product ideas, and serve as a reference to understand the impact and response of changes on real users.
